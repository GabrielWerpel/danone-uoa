------------------------------
 ERD (Entity Relationship Diagram)
------------------------------

Database: TeckInterview

Can
  - CanID (INT, Primary Key, Identity)
  - LineName (NVARCHAR(100), Nullable)

Product
  - ProductID (INT, Primary Key, Identity)
  - ProductName (NVARCHAR(100), NOT NULL)
  - ProductCode (NVARCHAR(50), Nullable)

Operator
  - OperatorID (INT, Primary Key, Identity)
  - OperatorName (NVARCHAR(100), NOT NULL)

WeightMeasurement
  - MeasurementID (INT, Primary Key, Identity)
  - CanID (INT, Foreign Key to Can)
  - ProductID (INT, Foreign Key to Product)
  - OperatorID (INT, Foreign Key to Operator)
  - DateTime (DATETIME, NOT NULL)
  - Weight (DECIMAL(10,3), NOT NULL)
  - Shift (NVARCHAR(20), Nullable)
  - Comments (NVARCHAR(255), Nullable)
